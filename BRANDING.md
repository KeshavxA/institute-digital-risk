# Institute of Digital Risk - Branding & Vision

## 🎨 Design Philosophy
The branding for the Institute of Digital Risk is built on the pillars of **Structure, Resilience, and Innovation**. 

### 🧊 Logo Concept
The cube-inspired icon symbols the solid foundation required in risk management. Its interlocking geometric lines represent the complexity of digital systems, while the unified structure suggests the resilience provided by expert oversight and education.

### 🎨 Color Palette
- **Innovation Orange**: Represents energy, creativity, and the forward-thinking nature of digital transformation.
- **Authority Black**: Symbolizes stability, professional rigor, and the protective nature of risk governance.
- **Precision White**: Provides clarity and focus, mirroring the precision required in technical education.

### 🖋️ Typography & Rationale
The typography utilizes a clean, high-contrast sans-serif font to communicate modern professionalism and academic clarity. This choice ensures legibility across digital platforms while maintaining a "tech-first" aesthetic that resonates with both students and industry professionals.
